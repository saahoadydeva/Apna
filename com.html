Cyber Hack

A Bug Bounty Platform

At Cyber Hack, our goal is to develop and operate a bug bounty platform that provides a platform for companies and organizations to receive and manage reports of potential security vulnerabilities in their websites, applications, and other online systems.

Our objective is to offer rewards to individuals who find and report vulnerabilities, and to provide support and assistance to customers in managing and mitigating these vulnerabilities.

Our motto is "Empowering security researchers to make the internet a safer place," and our target is to become a trusted and respected provider of bug bounty services, just like Hackerone and Bugcrowd.

If you are interested in joining our team or using our platform, please visit our website at bjb to learn more and contact us.
